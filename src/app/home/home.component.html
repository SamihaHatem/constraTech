Egypt's Most Influential Construction Event: Showcasing Innovation and Forging Strategic Partnerships

Under the patronage of the Ministry of Housing, Utilities & Urban Communities, the 6th edition of Big 5 Construct Egypt was a resounding success. Over 18,258 construction experts and more than 287 exhibitors from 20 countries participated, showcasing cutting-edge innovations and technologies in the construction sector.

The event fostered business networking and stakeholder collaboration, resulting in the formation of new strategic partnerships. Live demonstrations captivated visitors and opened doors to significant business opportunities through the latest technological advancements.
